## Core Zerg Principles

Zerg macro-play rests on three pillars:

- **Inject discipline** — Queen injects every 29 seconds. Missing injects costs larva and production.
- **Creep spread** — Creep highways grant +30% movement speed. All attacks should flow over creep.
- **Economic aggression** — Drone aggressively early; expand before spending on army unless threatened.

> Rule of thumb: If your mineral bank exceeds 400, you are either not droning or not expanding.

## ZvT — Zerg vs Terran

### Opening: 17 Hatch / 17 Pool / 19 Gas

```
13 - Overlord
17 - Hatchery (natural)
17 - Spawning Pool
19 - Extractor
20 - Queen x2
22 - Zergling x4 (hold cyclone/reaper)
24 - Lair
```

### Mid-Game Goal (4:00–10:00)
- Establish 3 bases by 5:30
- Lair → Baneling Nest → Evolution Chamber x2
- Research: Zergling Speed, Melee +1, Carapace +1
- Build 16 Lings + 8 Banes as reactive defense

### Engaging Bio
Banelings are the key unit. Split zerglings wide; banelings engage the bio ball.
- Surround with lings, then roll banelings into the center
- Use Infestor Fungal Growth to immobilize bio before banelings land

### Countering Mech
Switch to Roach-Ravager to crack Tank lines.
- Ravager Corrosive Bile forces Tanks to unsiege
- Follow with Roach flood + Zergling run-bys at mineral lines

## ZvZ — Zerg vs Zerg

### Opening: Pool First (Aggressive)

```
13 - Overlord
14 - Spawning Pool
16 - Extractor
17 - Hatchery
18 - Queen
20 - Zergling x4
22 - Roach Warren
```

### Key ZvZ Principle
- Zerg vs Zerg is won by **information**. Always have an overlord over the enemy's natural.
- Roach-Ravager beats Ling-Bane in equal fights; Ling-Bane beats Roach in open ground.
- Do **not** overcommit to hatch-first if the opponent went pool-first — you will be overwhelmed.

### Ravager Timing
At 4:30, a 6 Roach → 4 Ravager all-in can win the game outright if scouted no counter.
Corrosive Bile the opposing hatchery; kill drones while natural is denied.

## ZvP — Zerg vs Protoss

### Opening: 17 Hatch / 18 Pool

```
13 - Overlord
17 - Hatchery
18 - Spawning Pool
19 - Extractor x2
20 - Roach Warren
24 - Lair
```

### Dealing with Protoss Aggression
- **Adept shades**: Pre-place spine crawlers at natural ramp; pull 4 drones to help
- **Immortal-Chargelot**: Lurkers are the answer — burrow at natural, siege the attack
- **Colossus**: Corruptors + Hydra; target Colossus first with Corruptor Corruption

### Late ZvP: Hydra-Lurker-Broodlord
- 3 base economy → Hydralisk Den → Lurker Den → Lurker Aspect
- Hold 3rd base with Lurkers; use Hydra-Ling-Bane for counter-attacks
- Once Hive is up: Spire → Corruptors → Brood Lords
- Brood Lord + Infestor ball is near-unstoppable if properly executed
